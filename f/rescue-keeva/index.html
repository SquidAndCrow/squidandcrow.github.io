Rescue Keeva

According to Loranna Steve the Crime Lord has been holding her entire family hostage. Keeva has been forced to remain Steve's fence and Loranna has worked as his forger. Their parents are being held hostage by Steve's crew

Loranna has been forging maps detailing the location of the Portal of Carcossa, leading would be adventurers right into Steve's ambushes. She knows the true location of the Portal, and will provide it to the party if they can bring back her family.

Stakes

  1. Will the party help Loranna?
  2. Will Captain Sven take over the tavern quarter?
  3. Will the PCs ever be allowed to return to Bagnan?
